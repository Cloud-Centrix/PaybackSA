Frequently Asked Questions

How do I split a bill?

Tap the + button on the Bills tab, add people and items, then assign items to each person. PayBack SA calculates what everyone owes.

How do I track a trip?

Go to the Trips tab, create a new trip, add participants, and log expenses as they happen. The app tracks balances and shows who owes whom.

How do I share a bill summary?

Open a bill, tap Share, and choose WhatsApp, email, or another app. Your bank details will be included if you've saved them in Settings.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Settings in the app. If you see a premium upgrade prompt, tap Restore Purchase. This will check for any previous purchases linked to your app store account.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't upload your personal information to any server. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual bills and trips within the app. To remove all data, clear the app's data in your device settings, or uninstall the app.
