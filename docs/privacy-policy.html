Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 March 2026

PayBack SA ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application developed by Cloud Centrix. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use PayBack SA.

Summary: PayBack SA stores your data locally on your device. We do not collect personal information on our servers. The only external services that receive limited data are RevenueCat (purchases), Sentry (crash reports), and Expo (app updates).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Your name – used to personalise the app
  • Bank account details – account holder name, bank name, account number, and branch code, used for generating payment summaries
  • Bill & trip data – names of participants, item descriptions, amounts, expense categories, and payment status
  • Receipt photos – photos taken or selected for receipt scanning

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash reports – error messages and stack traces sent to Sentry when the app encounters an error (production only, 20% sampling rate)
  • Purchase data – premium subscription status managed by RevenueCat
  • App update checks – app version information sent to Expo update services

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All personal data (your name, bank details, bills, trips, and participant information) is stored locally on your device only using encrypted device storage. We do not upload, sync, or store this data on any external server.

Receipt photos remain on your device and are not transmitted to external services.

3. How Your Data Is Shared

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data is shared only in these circumstances:

  • When you choose to share – the app lets you share bill/trip summaries via WhatsApp, email, or other apps. These summaries may include participant names, amounts owed, and your bank details if you have saved them. You control when and with whom you share.
  • Crash reporting (Sentry) – anonymous error data is sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs. This does not include your personal data, names, or financial information.
  • In-app purchases (RevenueCat) – purchase and subscription status is managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • App updates (Expo) – your app version is checked against Expo's update service to deliver improvements.

4. Permissions

  • Camera – used to photograph receipts for bill scanning. Camera access is requested only when you use the receipt scanning feature and can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual bills or trips within the app
  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)

6. Children's Privacy

PayBack SA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's secure storage
  • API keys and sensitive configuration are stored securely and not exposed in the app bundle
  • Crash reports are anonymised and do not contain personal data

8. South African POPIA Compliance

In terms of the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA):

  • We process personal information only for the purpose of providing bill-splitting and expense-tracking services
  • Your data is stored on your device and under your control
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time
  • We will notify you of any material changes to this privacy policy

9. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat – in-app purchase management – Privacy Policy
  • Sentry – crash reporting – Privacy Policy
  • Expo – app updates – Privacy Policy

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
